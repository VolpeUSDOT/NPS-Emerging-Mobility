Bryce Canyon Shuttle Information
  • The park shuttle is free with park admission. In Bryce Canyon City, park passes can be purchased at the Shuttle Station or Ruby's Inn Adventure Center.
  • Oversized vehicles (longer than 23 feet) are not allowed at the Visitor Center or main viewpoint parking lots during shuttle hours. Park your RV and ride the shuttle.
  • While the shuttle is not required, it provides the most predictable way to enjoy the Bryce Amphitheater while also helping reduce congestion, noise, and air pollution.
  • Plan Accordingly: Last Shuttle begins route from Shuttle Station at 7:30 PM

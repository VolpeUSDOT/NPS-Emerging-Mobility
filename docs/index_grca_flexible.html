Village Route Shuttle Information
  • The park shuttle is free with park admission. Park passes can be purchased at the Visitor Center or Backcountry Information Center.
  • While the shuttle is not required, it provides the most predictable way to enjoy the canyon views of the South Rim while also helping reduce congestion, noise, and air pollution.
  • Plan Accordingly: To catch the last bus, be at a bus stop no later than 8:45 PM
